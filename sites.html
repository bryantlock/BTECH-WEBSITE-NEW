Welcome

Students attending Business Technology Early College High School (BTECH) will learn how to help the world run better using technology to solve everyday world problems. Our students will become expert readers, writers, and critical thinkers with the technical skills to be successful in our modern information-based global economy.

Values

BTECH Core Values

We believe for students to succeed professionally and academically, they need an education based on technology, innovation, tihnking and problem solving. C.R.A.F.T stands for, Collbaboration, Responsiveness, Advocacy, Focus, Tenacity

Deal

6 Year Program

BTECHs 6 year program is a dual enrollment program with QCC. Students will take college courses along with regular high school courses. As students progress to the 11th grade, they choose between two paths of Technology and Business.
